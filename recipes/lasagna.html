Lasagna

Homemade Lasagna is a classic recipe that every cook should have in their rotation.

The best lasagna recipe is made with ingredients that you know, and it’s not difficult at all! All you’ll need for this easy lasagna recipe is one pan, one bowl, and a 9×13 baking dish!

Ingredients

  • cheese
  • garlic
  • beef & sausage
  • pasta sauce
  • mozzarella
  • onion
  • tomato paste
  • parmesan
  • parsley
  • lasagna noodles

Steps

  1. Step one:

    Combine egg, flour, and parsley in a bowl. Combine sauces in a separate bowl. Combine meats in a separate bowl.

  2. Step two:

    Cook the meat in a pan with garlic, add sauce to pan and let simmer.

  3. Step three:

    Layer noodles with cheese and meat sauce. Let it bake in the oven until it becomes crispy.
