Beef Stew

Amazing Beef Stew Recipe

The weather is cooling. The leaves are changing. This is the time of year when everyone starts craving comforting, hearty recipes to warm themselves from the inside out.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • 3 Carrots
  • Tomato Paste
  • 2 Onions
  • Beef Broth
  • potatos
  • cilantro

Steps

  1. Step one:

    Sear meat and let rest in separate bowl.

  2. Step two:

    Sear veggies in the same pan and add tomato paste.

  3. Step three:

    Add beef back into pan and sprinkle with flour, stir until evenly mixed.

  4. Step four:

    Add broth and cilantro and let braise in the oven for 2 hours.
