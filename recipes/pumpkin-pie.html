Pumpkin Pie Recipe

Fall is the best time for your favorite pumpkin dessert.

Ingredients

  • 7 eggs
  • Whipped cream
  • Flour
  • Confectioners sugar
  • Butter
  • 1, 2lb butternut pumpkin
  • Brown sugar
  • Sweetened condensed milk
  • Nutmeg
  • ginger

Steps

  1. Step one:

    Combine flour, confectioner's sugar, butter, and water

  2. Step two:

    Add dough to pie pan and coat with egg

  3. Step three:

    Cut pumpkin flesh into half inch cubes, heat in oven at 350 for 35 minutes

  4. Step four:

    Once the pumpkin is cool, blend with spices and add filling to the pan and let it bake for 1 more hour.
